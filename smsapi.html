If you have a WordPress eCommerce website , you can simply install our plugin and start sending SMS.

You can also use out API to send from your custom website or APP . You simply Need to Generate a token for the domain and you are good to go. Refer to our Getting Started With Our SMS API guide.

How Much Does It COST:

The SMS GATEWAY is provided for FREE with all your domains. If your domain is not registered with us , you can simply verify your ownership and start sending

How many SMS can I send:

We did not sent a limit, but it is Not UNLIMTED

What Kind Of Message Can I Send:

You can only Send from our predefined Templates using a FREE alphanumeric sender ID. We believe the templates provided address most use cases. If you think we missed something critical , Let us Know

If you need to compose Custom SMS , BULK SMS, or have your own Custom Sender ID we can help you set up with our partner easily.

If you are an established business and SMS adds a real value to your business, please consider working with our Partner to implement a proper SMS strategy.
